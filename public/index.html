Frequently Asked Questions

Why get Gutters anyways?
Gutters are essential for protecting your home's foundation, preventing soil erosion, protecting your landscaping, and avoiding water damage to your walls and basement. They direct rainwater away from your home, preventing costly water damage that could lead to foundation cracks, basement flooding, or mold growth. In the long run, a quality gutter system is a crucial investment in protecting your home's value and structural integrity.
Should I have 5 inch or 6 inch gutters?
The choice between 5" and 6" gutters depends on several factors. 5" gutters are standard for most homes and work well for average rainfall areas. However, 6" gutters are recommended if you have a steeper roof pitch, large roof area, or live in an area with heavy rainfall. 6" gutters can handle about 40% more water than 5" gutters. We can assess your specific situation and recommend the best size for your home during our free consultation.
How often should I clean my gutters?
We recommend cleaning your gutters at least twice a year - in spring and fall. However, if you have many trees near your home, you might need more frequent cleanings. Installing gutter guards can significantly reduce the need for regular cleaning.
What are the benefits of seamless gutters?
Seamless gutters offer numerous advantages: they have fewer leak points, provide better water flow, require less maintenance, and look more aesthetically pleasing. They're custom-fit to your home, reducing the risk of water damage and foundation issues.
Do you offer gutter guards? Are they worth it?
Yes, we offer both standard and premium gutter guards. They're definitely worth the investment as they prevent debris buildup, reduce maintenance needs, prevent ice dams in winter, and extend the life of your gutters. Our premium guards offer enhanced protection and durability.
How long does gutter installation take?
For most residential properties, we can complete a full gutter installation in one day. Larger homes or more complex installations might take 2-3 days. We always aim to minimize disruption to your daily routine while ensuring quality installation.
What type of warranty do you offer?
We stand behind our work with a warranty that covers labor up to 1 year. This warranty does not cover acts of God for example if a tree hits the gutter this would not be covered.
