FAQ

Straight answers.

Why get gutters anyway?

Gutters protect your home's foundation, prevent soil erosion, protect your landscaping, and keep water off your walls. They direct rainwater away from your home, preventing costly damage like foundation cracks or mold growth. In this part of Texas especially, a quality gutter system is a crucial investment in protecting your home's value and structural integrity.

Should I get 5″ or 6″ gutters?

5″ gutters are standard for most homes and work well in average rainfall. 6″ gutters are recommended for steeper roof pitches, large roof areas, or heavy-rainfall regions like ours — they handle about 40% more water. We'll assess your home and recommend the right size during a free consultation.

How often should I clean my gutters?

At least twice a year — spring and fall. If you have many trees near your home, you might need more frequent cleanings. Installing gutter guards can significantly reduce how often cleaning is needed.

What are the benefits of seamless gutters?

Fewer leak points, better water flow, less maintenance, and a cleaner look. They're custom-fabricated to fit your home exactly, reducing the risk of water damage and foundation issues.

Are gutter guards worth it?

Yes. We offer standard and premium guards. They prevent debris buildup, reduce maintenance, and extend the life of your gutters. Premium guards offer enhanced protection and durability.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in one day. Larger homes or more complex installations might take 2-3 days. We aim to minimize disruption to your routine while ensuring quality work.

What warranty do you offer?

We stand behind our work with a labor warranty covering up to 1 year. The warranty does not cover acts of God — for example, if a tree falls on the gutter.
