FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

How much does siding replacement cost in Missouri City?

It depends on the square footage and the material — Hardie, LP, vinyl, or cedar. Our online estimator gives you an all-in ballpark in about a minute; for partial repairs, just enter the affected area. We confirm everything on-site for free.

What's the best siding for the Houston area?

Fiber cement (Hardie-style) is the most popular here for good reason: it resists rot, fire, and pests, and handles our humidity and storms better than wood. We'll walk you through the trade-offs for your home and budget.

Can you just replace my rotted fascia?

Yes — and it's a specialty of ours. Fascia is the board your gutters mount to, and we see rotted fascia on gutter jobs constantly. We can replace just the damaged sections, and it's smart to do it before hanging new gutters.

Do I need to replace all my siding or just part?

Often just the damaged sections. We'll assess honestly during the on-site visit and recommend the smallest fix that actually solves the problem — not a full re-side you don't need.

Do you handle storm damage?

Yes. Houston-area storms are hard on siding and fascia, and we repair and replace storm-damaged exterior surfaces to seal your home back up.
